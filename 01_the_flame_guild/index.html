F.L.A.M.E

Fedration of Labourers, Artisans, Mages, & Engineers

"Keep the F.L.A.M.E of fairness burning!"

🔥Campaign: The Right to Rest (and Respawn)

We are tired of being tired. Mages, smiths, engineers, and potion brewers are working long hours without proper breaks. We're not asking for magic - just a moment to eat our stew in peace.

Our 3 main Campaign Goals

  1. The 4 hour rule strong - Every worker gets a break every 4 hours. Even during dragon sieges.
  2. The Universal Bedroll acess - Free flameproof bedrolls for all guild-certified workers
  3. Respawn Leave - If you're ressurected due to unsafe working conditions, you get the day off. No agruments.

Slogans You'll see Around Town

  • "Break before Blades!"
  • "No stews, no spells!"
  • "Stop the grind - nap time now!"
